Alambadi (Tamil Nadu)

A strong breed from Tamil Nadu, mainly used for plowing and draught purposes.

Amrit Mahal (Karnataka)

One of the best draught cattle breeds, known for speed and endurance.

Bachaur (Bihar)

A hardy breed known for disease resistance and adaptability.

Badri (Uttarakhand)

First registered indigenous cow breed of Uttarakhand, known for high-altitude survival.

Bargur (Tamil Nadu)

Primarily used for agricultural work and famous for its energetic nature.

Belahi (Haryana, Chandigarh)

A dual-purpose breed used for milk production and agriculture.

Binjharpuri (Orissa)

Known for its high fertility rate and moderate milk yield.

Brownsind (Allahabad)

A hybrid breed developed from Red Sindhi and Brown Swiss cattle.

Cutchi (Gujarat)

A well-adapted breed with high resistance to extreme climatic conditions.

Dangi (Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh)

Strong and disease-resistant breed, ideal for hilly terrain.

Gir (Gujarat)

One of the best dairy breeds, known for high-quality milk production.

Hallikar (Karnataka)

Preferred for bullock cart racing and agricultural tasks.

Hariana (Haryana, UP, Rajasthan)

A popular breed for both dairy and draught purposes.

Tharparkar (Rajasthan)

Resistant to extreme heat, known for high-quality milk production.
