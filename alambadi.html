Alambadi (Tamil Nadu)

The Alambadi breed originates from Tamil Nadu and is well-known for its strength and endurance, making it suitable for plowing and draught purposes.

Physical Characteristics:

  • Color: Grey or dark brown with white patches
  • Build: Medium-sized, muscular
  • Horns: Long and curved upwards
  • Hump: Well-developed

Productivity & Utility:

  • Used for agriculture and cart pulling
  • Bulls are highly resistant to harsh weather conditions
  • Not primarily a dairy breed, but can produce 2-4 liters of milk per day
