Belahi (Haryana, Chandigarh)

Origin: Primarily found in Haryana and Chandigarh regions of India.

Characteristics: Belahi cows are known for their excellent adaptability, good milk production, and resistance to harsh climatic conditions.

Physical Traits: They are medium-sized cows with a reddish-brown or light brown coat. They have a compact body structure and strong legs, making them suitable for both dairy and draft purposes.

Unique Features: Belahi cows produce milk with a high-fat content, making it ideal for dairy products like ghee and butter. They are also known for their hardiness and low maintenance requirements.

Uses: Primarily used for milk production, but also used in farming for draft work due to their strength and endurance.
