Bargur (Tamil Nadu)

Origin: Bargur Hills, Tamil Nadu, India

Characteristics: The Bargur breed is known for its agility, speed, and high endurance. It is primarily used for plowing and draught purposes, making it an excellent choice for hilly and rugged terrains.

Physical Traits: Bargur cows have a compact and lean body, usually reddish-brown with white patches. They have sharp, backward-curving horns and are highly active and energetic.

Unique Features: This breed is well-adapted to the forested regions of Tamil Nadu and is highly resistant to local diseases. The Bargur cattle are known to be aggressive, requiring skilled handling.

Uses: Primarily used for agricultural purposes due to their strength and agility. The milk yield is moderate, but the breed is valued more for its work capabilities.
